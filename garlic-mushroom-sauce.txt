Ingrediants:
250 g brown mushrooms
100 g salted butter
250 ml fresh cream
3 cloves garlic
salt and petter to season

Method:
1. Chop the mushrooms into 1 cm strips
2. Chop the garlic up finely
3. Melt the butter over medium heat
4. Add the garlic and let simmer
5. Add the muchrooms and stir gently until moisture leaves mushrooms
6. Add the cream
7. Reduce over 30 mins while simmering, stirring occasionally
8. Season when thickened and serve hot
